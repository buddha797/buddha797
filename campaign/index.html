Hi! I'm Jacob Hicks

and I'm running for College Council President. I'm running to reform College Council and represent the interests of every student in Emory College. Every year students (like me!) tell you how they're going to change student government. Very rarely do we ever come close to doing everything in our platforms. That's why you can find a detailed plan and timeline for every single one of my initiatives (and even the bills I will present) on this website. I want to be a voice for every Emory student, so please get in touch!
